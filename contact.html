Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my cake order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3-5 days in advance for regular cakes and 1-2 weeks for custom or special occasion cakes.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15km radius of our store. Delivery fees vary based on distance. For orders outside this area, please contact us for special arrangements.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan options. Please specify your dietary requirements when placing your order.

How do I place a custom order?

You can place a custom order by filling out our contact form, calling us, or visiting our store. We'll discuss your requirements and provide a quote.
