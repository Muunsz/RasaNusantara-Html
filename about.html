Our Story

Aloha Zakia was founded in 2020 by Zakia Rahman, a passionate baker with a dream to bring joy through delicious desserts. What started as a small home kitchen operation has now grown into a beloved dessert shop known for its unique flavors and exceptional quality.

The name "Aloha Zakia" combines the Hawaiian greeting that embodies love and peace with our founder's name, representing our commitment to creating desserts with love and sharing the joy they bring.
